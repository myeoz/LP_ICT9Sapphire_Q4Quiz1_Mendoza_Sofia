NOLI ME TANGERE CHARACTERS

Ibarra
  • Full name:Juan Crisostomo Ibarra Y Magsalin
  • Son of Don Rafael
  • Studied in Europe for 7 years
  • Love intereset of Maria Clara

Maria Clara
  • Believed to be the daughter of Captain Tiago and the Goddaughter of Father Damaso
  • It is revealed later in the book that Father Damaso is actually the biological father of Maria
  • Love interest of Ibarra

Father Damaso
  • Main Antagonist
  • Corrupt Spanish Fransiscan Priest
  • Believed to be the goddfather of Maria Clara
  • It is revealed later in the book that he is the biological father of Maria
  • Mastermind behind Don Rafael's death

Elias
  • He is known to be the mysterious friend of Ibarra.
  • He is very patriotic and wants to take revolutionary stance.
  • Against the Spanish colonial government and the Catholic Church

Captain Tiago
  • Full Name: Don Santiago De Los Santos
  • Believed to be the father of Maria Clara
  • Richest man in Binondo
  • Owns a house in “Kalye Analoague” where multiple gatherings take place in
  • Has close ties with high-ranked members of the Catholic Church

Aunt Isabel
  • Cousin of Captain Tiago
  • Mother figure of Maria Clara

Father Salvi
  • A sly and witty Spanish priest
  • He has an eye for Father Damaso's post as friar curate of San Diego
  • He is also secretly in love with Maria Clara

Don Rafael Ibarra
  • Father of Crisostomo Ibarra
  • He was put in jail for a crime he didn't do and died before his name was cleared.
  • Hated by Father Damaso who accused him of sedition and heresy.

Pilosopo Tasyo
  • Also known as Don Anastasio
  • He is an old man who is known to be crazy in his community.
  • Has an educational background in Philosophy.
  • Gave Ibarra advice and helped Don Rafael in the past.

Doña Consolacion
  • Wife of Alperes
  • A Filipino, but is ashamed of it so she claims that she can't speak Tagalog even if it's her mother tongue.
  • She is awful to her husband and others.
  • She has so much pride that she expects others to respect her.

Sisa
  • Mother of Crispin and Basilio
  • Worships her husband as a God despite the way he horribly treats her.
  • She is the typical Filipina wife who shows unconditional love to her children.
  • Went mad when she lost her children and often called for them in the streets.

Basilio
  • Eldest son of Sisa.
  • Older brother of Crispin.
  • Just like his brother, he works as a sexton.
  • Ran away from home to find his brother but failed.

Crispin
  • Youngest son of Sisa.
  • Younger brother of Basilio.
  • Just like his brother, he works as a sexton.
  • Kept as prisoner for a debt he does not owe.

Alperes
  • Husband of Dona Consolacion who he fights regularly with.
  • Father of Basilio and Crispin.
  • Head of the Civil Guard in San Diego.
  • A drunkard and has fueds with Father Salvi.

Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • Full name: Donya Victorina de los reyes de Espadaña
  • Wife of Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • Wears tons of make-up to not only look younger but to look like she is of Spanish heritage.
  • Wants to marry off her nephew, Linares, to Maria Clara to increase her social rank.

Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • Husband of Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • He is a con artist and a fraud
  • Calls himself a doctor but has no medical experience
  • He demands excessively prices for his services. When his patients find out about his schemes, he moves to a new location where he is unknown.
