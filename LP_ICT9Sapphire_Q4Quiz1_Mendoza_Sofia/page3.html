Consisting of over 7000 islands and islets, the Philippines is a great place to spend your next vacation! With its wonderful beaches,
warm-hearted and hospitable natives, beautiful scenery and nature the Philippines is also rich in culture!
Visit these wonderful tourist spots to find more about this beautiful country!

Palawan

Palawan is best known for its pristine beaches and lagoons. Consisting of no less than 1,780 islands and islets, Palawan's capital, Puerto Princesa is actually one of the cleanest cities you'll find in the Philippines! Places like Coron and El Nido are also very famous tourist destinations for dive sites and karst scenery.

Albay, Bicol

The province of Albay is frequently visited in the Bicol Region for it houses the famous Mayon Volcano! The volcano is known for its perfect cone shape and height. Here you can also find rich heritage and culture like historic churches and ruins!

Banaue

Home of the Ifugaos, Banaue is also home to the famous rice terraces. It is now recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Visit Banaue for the gentle-hearted Ifugao people, cool weather, beautiful mountain ranges, rice terraces, and rich culture!
